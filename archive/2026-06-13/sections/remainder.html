Innefu Labs Raises $30 Million for AI-Powered National Security and Intelligence Analytics

Innefu Labs, an Indian AI company specializing in national security applications — intelligence analytics, cyber threat detection, and surveillance — raised $30 million in a Series B round from Singapore-based growth equity firm Panthera Growth Partners. The company works primarily with Indian government agencies and defense establishments.

Why it matters: AI for national security is a high-growth, high-stakes segment that rarely gets discussed alongside consumer or enterprise AI — but it is one of the fastest-growing AI deployment domains globally. The Singapore funding signals international investor interest in India's defense tech sector, which sits at the intersection of AI capability and sovereign security. For an AI generalist mapping the full landscape of where AI is going, this is a segment worth understanding.
