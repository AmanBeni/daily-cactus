Sarvam AI hits unicorn status on $234M raise led by HCLTech; Razorpay quietly files for IPO

Sarvam AI has closed a $234 million funding round led by HCLTech, pushing the Bengaluru company past the $1 billion valuation mark. The funds are earmarked for advanced AI foundation model development and India's sovereign AI ecosystem. Simultaneously, Razorpay has confidentially filed its DRHP — a significant step toward a domestic IPO.

Why it matters: Sarvam is India's most credible bet on sovereign AI infrastructure at the model development layer, not just the application wrapper. A unicorn valuation with HCLTech's enterprise distribution behind it changes the competitive calculus for India's AI sector meaningfully. The Razorpay filing, if it proceeds, will be the most-watched fintech IPO of the year.

Zerodha, Groww, Angel One and Upstox get IFSCA nod to offer US stock trading

India's four largest retail brokerages have been granted approval by the International Financial Services Centre Authority to enable their customers to buy US equities directly through their platforms. The move opens cross-border equity access for India's 100 million-plus retail investor base through apps they already use daily.

Why it matters: This is a structural market opening — Indian retail investors who want US tech exposure can now access it through familiar, regulated platforms rather than workarounds like Vested or INDMoney. It also signals meaningful progress on capital account liberalisation and GIFT City's financial hub ambitions.

BigBasket founding CEO Hari Menon exits; Tata Digital installs Amazon veteran Amit Nanda

Hari Menon and Vipul Parekh have stepped down from executive roles at BigBasket, transitioning to board advisory positions. Amazon veteran Amit Nanda takes over as CEO. The transition ends the founder-led era of one of India's original e-grocery pioneers in the middle of an intensifying quick-commerce battle with Blinkit, Zepto, and Swiggy Instamart.

Why it matters: Tata is professionalising BigBasket management at exactly the moment quick commerce threatens to eat its lunch. The appointment of an Amazon logistics executive signals where Tata thinks the battle will be won or lost — on operations and fulfilment, not product innovation.

Turtlemint's losses grew 19% to ₹185 Cr — and the insurtech is filing for IPO anyway

Insurtech startup Turtlemint reported a 19% year-on-year jump in net losses to ₹185 crore as it moves toward a public listing. The company's IPO window is approaching. Widening losses ahead of an IPO invite public market scrutiny of its unit economics and growth-versus-profitability trade-off.

Why it matters: With several insurtech plays heading to public markets, the Turtlemint numbers are a useful calibration of how much loss the market will absorb at what valuation. Read the upcoming DRHP closely before forming a view.

Wealth management startup CREST emerges from stealth with $3.1M pre-seed round

CREST has raised $3.1 million (₹29.3 crore) in a pre-seed round led by Atrium Ventures and BEENEXT, exiting stealth mode. The startup is targeting the underserved affluent-but-not-ultra-HNI segment — the ₹50L–₹5Cr wealth bracket — with a tech-driven wealth management platform.

Why it matters: India's wealth management gap is real: sophisticated products exist for UHNIs, and near nothing good exists for the tier below. A pre-seed at $3.1M is small, but the investor list is credible and the segment is large. One to watch.

YourNest VC closes Rs 400 Cr Continuum Fund for deep tech and enterprise software

YourNest Venture Capital has raised Rs 400 crore for its Continuum Fund, focused on deep tech and B2B software startups. The close represents continued LP appetite for Indian deep tech despite the broader funding caution that hit consumer startups over the past two years.

Why it matters: A dedicated Rs 400 Cr fund for deep tech signals where institutional capital in India is looking next. For founders positioning in AI, climate tech, or enterprise SaaS, YourNest's portfolio decisions over the next 18 months will be worth tracking as a leading indicator.
