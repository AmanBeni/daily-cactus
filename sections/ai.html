Anthropic Becomes First AI Company to Join the Frontier Carbon Removal Coalition

Anthropic joined the Frontier coalition, a group of companies committing advance-market purchases to fund carbon removal. The coalition received another $915M in pledges this week, bringing total commitments over $3 billion. Anthropic is the first AI-native company in the group.

Key takeaway: Anthropic is staking out “responsible AI” territory at a moment when AI companies are under scrutiny for energy use.

Why it matters: For Indian AI startups watching how global leaders manage ESG optics alongside growth, this is a positioning playbook worth noting.
